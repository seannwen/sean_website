      Ergonomic Product Design

      Motivation

      The goal of this project was redesign the existing dumpster to highly improved the terrible condition and efficiently reduced the low back injury problem and decreased the energy consumption during the lifting load.

      Methodology

      First of all, our team identified the actual condition and generated the House of Quality table to obtain the engineering requirements and the customer needs. Secondly, we modeled our new design by using SolidWorks and import new product into Siemens Jack software to simulate the stress condition between original and new product. In addition, we calculated our new design by using NIOSH lifting equation to analyze the L4/L5 which is the most prominent area of the spine during lifting loads. Thus, we could come up with the comparison between the original and our new design. The simulation would focus on 50th percentile USA and Japanese manikins with male and female.

      Result

      New design made three extra gates in front of the dumpster and was lower than the lids, which was located on top of the dumpster. The height of the barriers could reduce the L4/L5 spine suffer high pressure while lifting the loads over customerâ€™s head. The new design might increase the cost to make additional three gates, but it will lower the injury happen.
